# Pricing & packaging (instructions.md §30, Annexe E)

- **Mode Équipe (MVP)**: per-seat, org-wide. Entry for TN accounts.
- **Mode Personnel (grands comptes)**: per-user, fine governance.
- **Units** (Annexe E): TN self-hosted 60-150k TND/yr (50-200 capped seats);
  EU mid-market 20-50k€/yr. Target gross margin 55-70% (caching + routing).
- **Cost guard**: LLM cost ≤ 10% of price (native budgets §10.2, §25).
- **Billing**: TND + local VAT, invoices downloadable; usage split interactive vs
  scheduled (`usage_daily.origin`), 14-day graph with cache hit rate (§4.4).
- **The 4 valuation metrics** (Annexe E): net retention ≥100%, gross margin ≥60%,
  FX-currency ARR share, qualified pipeline ≥3× target ARR at M24.
